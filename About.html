ABOUT US

The Sojourner was created by a group friends in a small, cramped college dorm room. We have always been fascinated by the inner workings of the music industry and what makes it tick. Undergoing the colossal task of researching mainstream acts and independent acts alike, we've come to grow into our own and create content that cater to people from all walks of life. Since then, we've expanded to cover the latest musical acts and other music news.

WE ARE THE SOJOURNER

MEET THE FOUNDER

Isaac Newmanfounded the organization, which now employs 300 employees and two other subsidiaries, to explore the and expand the depths of music journalism. He envisioned a cultural hub for independent artists to thrive in lieu of mainstream media's aversion to "non-marketable" acts. Isaac and The Sojourner have made every step to make this possible.
